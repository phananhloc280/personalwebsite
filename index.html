My Activities Album

OISP CAMP

OISP Camp is a traditional activity of the Office for International Study Programs (OISP), organized by first-year students under the guidance of senior students who were members of the previous Organizing Committee. The purpose of OISP Camp is to enhance unity, foster bonding, and showcase the uniqueness of each class and the entire cohort.

OISP YOUTH CAMP

The program series takes place annually in April to celebrate Youth Month, promoting youth knowledge, leadership skills, and fostering solidarity among Youth Union and other club organizations.

OISP GALA

The "OISP GALA" night is an annual event that takes place before the Lunar New Year holiday. It is an opportunity for OISP students, Youth Union officials, club members, and the Management Board to come together, reflect on the past year, and share their achievements.

BREAKING RECORDS

Breaking Records is a music competition open to all students of the University of Technology, known as the first of its kind at the Office for International Study Programs (OISP). The contest spans two months, featuring various captivating rounds for passionate music enthusiasts and singers to showcase their talents.

OISP INTERNATIONAL FESTIVAL

OISP International Festival offers a vibrant platform for students, especially international ones, with diverse activities such as country showcases and multicultural performances. It creates a meaningful and enjoyable experience outside of academic studies.

100 NGÀY BAY

"100 Ngày Bay" is a fundraising program with various activities such as fundraising sales and charity concerts. Its mission is to support underprivileged students in remote areas, providing them with better learning environments.
